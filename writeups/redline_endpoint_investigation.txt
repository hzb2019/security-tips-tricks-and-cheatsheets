
Can you identify the product name of the machine?
  Select "System Information" from the left side. Look under "Operating System Information".

Can you find the name of the note left on the Desktop for the "Charles"?
  Check the "File System" and navigate to C:\Users\charles\Desktop, then right click "Desktop"
  and select "Toggle Selected Item Only".

Find the Windows Defender service; what is the name of its service DLL? 
  Go to "Windows Services" on the left side and use the regex search for "defender".
  Scroll to the right to find the "Service DLL" column.

The user manually downloaded a zip file from the web. Can you find the filename? 
  Go to "File Download History" on the left panel and use regex search for "zip". Another 
  useful search could have been for "Manual" in the "Download Type" column.

Provide the filename of the malicious executable that got dropped on the user's Desktop.
  Go to "Timeline", then search for "C:\Users\charles\Desktop" in the "Summary" column.

Provide the MD5 hash for the dropped malicious executable.
  Take from the "MD5" column in the row found above.

What is the name of the ransomware? 
  Look at the text before ".exe" in the malicious executable.
